About Acadmay

Built for the realities of Indian education

Acadmay was founded on a simple belief: that school administrators shouldn't have to spend half their day on paperwork, disconnected spreadsheets, and manual fee collection.

Our mission

Digitalise every school operation — without complexity

We saw the gap between what school management software promised and what it actually delivered in practice. Most products were either too expensive, too complex, or built for a Western school system that doesn't match how Indian institutions operate.

Acadmay is different. It's designed ground-up for the Indian context — cash fee payments, regional boards, transport billing by zone, term-based academic calendars, and multi-stakeholder visibility that parents actually trust.

Our mission is to give every school — from a 200-student institute to a 2,000-student campus — the same operational quality that only the largest institutions could previously afford.

What we believe

Our core values

01

Schools first

Every product decision starts with one question: does this make a school administrator's or teacher's day meaningfully easier? If not, it doesn't ship.

02

Trust through transparency

Parents and students deserve real visibility into their financial records, attendance, and academic progress — not just summaries provided at the school's convenience.

03

Built for India

Offline payments, cash verification, regional board structures, Hindi/English interfaces — we design for how Indian schools actually work, not how Western ERP vendors assume they do.

04

Data security

Student and financial data is sensitive. Multi-tenant isolation, encrypted storage, and audited access logs are non-negotiable — not optional enterprise add-ons.

05

Continuous improvement

The platform evolves with every school we onboard. Features are driven by real operational feedback, not product roadmap theatre.

06

Accessible pricing

Quality school management software shouldn't require an enterprise budget. Our pricing is designed to be reachable for schools at every stage of growth.

How we're built

Architectural pillars

Unified data architecture

All modules share one data layer — attendance feeds into the admin dashboard, fees connect to student profiles, and academic records flow to parent portals without any manual sync.

True multi-tenancy

Each school is fully isolated at the subdomain level. No school can ever see another school's data — technically enforced, not just a policy promise.

Security by default

Role-based access control, encrypted data at rest and in transit, and a full audit log on every financial transaction — built into the platform, not bolted on.

Mobile-first access

Every interface — from the admin dashboard to the parent portal — is fully responsive. No app download required; it works perfectly from any smartphone browser.

The story

Why Acadmay exists

School administration in India is still largely manual. Fee registers, paper attendance rolls, handwritten report cards, and phone calls to absent students — these aren't edge cases, they're the norm even in well-funded institutions.

"The problem wasn't that good software didn't exist. It was that the software that existed wasn't built for how schools in India actually operate — the payment patterns, the board structures, the offline workflows."

Acadmay was built to close that gap. Not by asking schools to change how they work, but by building software that meets them where they are — and systematically upgrades every manual process into a digital, auditable, real-time pipeline.

We're a platform that started from first principles: what does a school principal actually need to see every morning? What does a parent need to trust that their child's education is on track? What does a teacher need to spend less time on paperwork and more time teaching?
