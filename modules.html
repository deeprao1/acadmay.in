Administration & Academic Control

The command centre for school leadership. Manage every student, staff member, curriculum, and schedule from a single, unified board — with real-time data flowing throughout.

01

Student lifecycle

Digital admission registration, profile management, section assignment, and historical academic portfolio tracking.

02

Curriculum maps

Academic heads define chapter timelines per subject and grade. Teachers log completion in real time.

03

Timetable builder

Visual drag-and-drop timetable creation with automatic conflict detection for rooms, teachers, and periods.

04

Exam planner

Schedule internal assessments, term exams, and board exams without clashes — with automated distribution.

05

Staff management

Employee records, department mapping, designation history, and leave balance tracking.

06

Holiday calendar

Centralised holiday planner with instant visibility for all stakeholders through their dashboards.

Automated Attendance & Check-In

Replace paper registers with a touchless, QR-powered attendance network. Absence alerts fire automatically. Every check-in is logged, timestamped, and auditable.

01

Dynamic QR tokens

Each session generates a unique, time-sensitive token. Tokens expire and cannot be shared or replayed.

02

Smartphone scanning

Any smartphone camera works as a check-in hub — no dedicated hardware required.

03

Instant absence alerts

Automated notifications to parents and administrators the moment an expected student is marked absent.

04

Compliance dashboard

Live attendance percentages by class, section, and individual student — with trend charts.

05

Staff check-in

Separate attendance tracking for teaching and non-teaching staff with punch-in/out logs.

06

Attendance reports

Daily, weekly, monthly, and term-wise reports exportable for regulatory compliance.

Secure Financial Ledger Suite

From custom invoice generation to offline payment verification — Acadmay's finance module is the most complete fee management system built for Indian schools.

01

Custom invoicing

Build fee structures by grade, section, transport zone, lab usage, and individual waivers.

02

Payment gateway

Direct Razorpay / payment gateway integration for online fee collection with instant receipts.

03

Offline verification desk

Cash and wire payments validated against reference slip IDs before ledger approval.

04

Scholarship tracking

Personal fee waivers and scholarship discounts applied at the student level with audit trail.

05

Defaulter tracking

Automated flagging of overdue accounts with escalation notifications to parents.

06

Financial reports

Revenue dashboards, collection summaries, and outstanding fee reports for management.

02

School onboarding
