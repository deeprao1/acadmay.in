Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025 · Effective date: January 1, 2025

Acadmay ("we", "our", "us") operates the acadmay.in platform and associated subdomains. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information about schools, administrators, teachers, students, and parents who use our platform.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, configure your institution, or contact our support team. This includes:

  • School and institutional details (name, address, board affiliation)
  • Administrator and staff contact information
  • Student records (names, class, section, admission numbers)
  • Fee transaction data and payment reference information
  • Attendance logs and academic assessment data

2. How we use your information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Operate and maintain the Acadmay platform for your institution
  • Process fee payments and generate receipts
  • Generate attendance reports and academic dashboards
  • Send operational notifications to administrators, teachers, and parents
  • Provide customer support and resolve technical issues
  • Improve the platform based on aggregate usage patterns

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Multi-tenant data isolation

Each school on the Acadmay platform operates in a fully isolated tenant environment. Data belonging to one institution is technically segregated from all other institutions and cannot be accessed by other tenants.

4. Data security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data storage, TLS encryption for all data in transit, role-based access controls, and audit logging for financial transactions. Access to student data is restricted to authenticated users within the institution.

5. Student data

We recognise that student data — particularly data relating to minors — requires heightened protection. We do not use student data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the educational management services contracted by the school. Student data is only accessible to authorised personnel within the institution.

6. Data retention

We retain institutional data for the duration of your Acadmay subscription plus 90 days after termination, during which time you may request an export of your data. After 90 days, data is permanently deleted from our systems.

7. Your rights

Institutions have the right to request a full export of their data, request correction of inaccurate data, and request deletion of data upon subscription termination. Contact us at privacy@acadmay.in for any privacy-related requests.

8. Changes to this policy

We will notify school administrators of material changes to this policy at least 30 days in advance via email.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@acadmay.in or visit our contact page.
